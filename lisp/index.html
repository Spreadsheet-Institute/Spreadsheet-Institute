S.I/Lisp/

"Single-function formulas."



Spreadsheet Lisp (SL), a pure subset of the Lisp programming paradigm, is designed to simplify spreadsheet formulas.

By aiming for "single-function formulas", Spreadsheet Lisp (SL) abstracts away unnecessary formula complexity in order to:

reduce formula sizes, thereby
improving formula readability, which
minimizes formula typos/mistakes and
multiplies formula workloads.
