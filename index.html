"Today's spreadsheet is tomorrow's abacus."



Spreadsheet Institute embodies a collaborative effort to seek, test, and break the boundaries of what can be accomplished by present & future spreadsheet technologies. Whether we're optimizing for performance, readability, extensibility, or even "mere aesthetics": our aim is to restore the spreadsheet experience itself from one of pain to one of pleasure, from the intellectually tedious to the intellectually tantalizing; in short, from a game of memory to a game of creativity.
